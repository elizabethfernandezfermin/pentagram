The Struggle for Freedom in La Española
Editorial, Book Design
Visual Resistance | Fall 2021

Engage with a captivating portfolio project commemorating the 500th anniversary of the first African slave revolt in the Americas. Working from a provided script, this collaborative effort involved three groups, with my role being in the Book Design team. The book beautifully documents the process of creating motion graphics, animations, and illustrations, showcasing my contributions such as the striking book cover, intricately designed map, and evocative depictions of moon phases. Additionally, my involvement extended to making typographic choices, designing chapter layouts, and curating the final draft, resulting in a powerful testament to the enduring fight for freedom.
