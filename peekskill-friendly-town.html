Peekskill: A Friendly Town
Branding, Digital Design
Digital Design Portfolio | Fall 2021

Embark on a journey through the revitalized city of Peekskill with this portfolio project. Experience the vibrant art, music, and downtown restaurant scene that has attracted a surge of visitors. Through a redesigned logo, captivating posters, and enchanting postcards featuring iconic landmarks, this project captures the essence of Peekskill's new era, inviting viewers to explore, indulge, and fall in love with this friendly town.
